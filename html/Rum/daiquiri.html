You got Daiquiri!

My Daiquiri in El Floridita - Ernest Hemingway

The Daiquiri is a classic rum-based cocktail that's known for its simplicity and refreshing taste. Originating in Cuba, it has become a favorite worldwide and is a perfect choice for those who appreciate the balance of sweet and sour flavors.

Ingredients
  • 2 oz White Rum
  • 3/4 oz Lime Juice
  • 2 bar spoons of sugar
  • (Optional) Lime wheel for garnish

Note: Garnish the Daiquiri with a lime wheel for a decorative touch and an extra hint of citrus aroma.

To make a Daiquiri, shake the white rum, lime juice, and sugar with ice. Strain into a chilled glass and garnish with a lime wheel. It's a cocktail that's easy to make and perfect for any occasion.

Enjoy your Daiquiri!
