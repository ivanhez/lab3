You got Mojito!

My Mojito in La Bodeguita - Ernest Hemingway

The Mojito is a refreshing cocktail that originated in Cuba. Its blend of mint, lime, and sweetness makes it a popular choice, especially on a warm day.

Ingredients
  • 2 oz White Rum
  • 1 oz Lime juice
  • 2 teaspoons of sugar
  • Mint leaves
  • Soda water
  • (Optional) Lime wedge for garnish

Note: Adjust the sugar to your taste preferences. Garnish with a lime wedge and a sprig of mint for an extra fresh touch.

To make a Mojito, muddle the mint leaves and sugar in a glass, add white rum and fresh lime juice. Fill the glass with ice, top it up with soda water, and stir gently. Garnish with a lime wedge and a sprig of mint.

Enjoy your Mojito!
