You got Negroni!

I think the Negroni is the perfect cocktail because it is three liquors that I don't like. - Anthony Bourdain

The Negroni is a classic Italian cocktail known for its perfect balance of strong, sweet, and bitter flavors. It's a popular choice among cocktail enthusiasts and has a rich history dating back to the early 20th century.

Ingredients
  • 1 oz Gin
  • 1 oz Vermouth
  • 1 oz Campari
  • (Optional) Orange peel for garnish

Note: The classic Negroni is typically garnished with an orange peel, expressed over the drink to release its oils, and then dropped into the glass.

To make a Negroni, simply mix the Gin, Vermouth, and Campari in a glass with ice. Stir well and garnish with an orange peel. It's a cocktail that's as simple to make as it is delightful to drink.

Enjoy your Negroni!
