You got Martini!

Shaken, not stirred. - how James Bond prefers his martini.

The Martini is a classic cocktail known for its elegance and simplicity. It's a timeless drink that has been a favorite among cocktail enthusiasts for decades.

Ingredients
  • 2 oz Gin
  • 1/2 oz Dry Vermouth
  • Ice cubes
  • Olive (no more than two)

Note: The Martini can also be prepared with different alochols as base or can be garnished with a lemon twist, depending on your preference.

To make a Martini, mix the Gin and Vermouth in a mixing glass with ice. Stir (or shake) well and strain into a chilled martini glass. Garnish with a lemon twist or an olive.

Enjoy your Martini!
