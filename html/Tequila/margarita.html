You got Margarita!

If life gives you limes, make margaritas. - Jimmy Buffet, singer-songwriter of hit song "Margaritaville"

The Margarita is a classic cocktail known for its perfect balance of sweet, sour, and salty flavors. It's a popular choice for those who enjoy a refreshing and tangy drink.

Ingredients
  • 2 oz Tequila
  • 1 oz Triple Sec
  • 3/4 oz Lime juice
  • Salt for rimming the glass
  • (Optional) Lime wheel

Note: Rim the glass with salt for an authentic Margarita experience. Garnish with a lime wheel for added freshness.

To make a Margarita, combine Tequila, Triple Sec, and fresh lime juice in a shaker with ice. Shake well and strain into a salt-rimmed glass. Garnish with a lime wheel.

Enjoy your Margarita!
