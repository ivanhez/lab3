The Tequila Sunrise is a vibrant and visually stunning cocktail that brings together tequila, orange juice, and grenadine. It's a perfect drink for sunny days and festive occasions.

Ingredients
  • 2 oz Tequila
  • 4 oz Orange Juice
  • 1/2 oz Grenadine
  • (Optional) Orange slice and cherry for garnish

Note: Pour the grenadine slowly over the back of a spoon or by drizzling it down the side of the glass to create the sunrise effect.

To make a Tequila Sunrise, fill a highball glass with ice, pour in the tequila and orange juice, then slowly pour the grenadine. Garnish with an orange slice and a cherry. Do not stir, let the colors create a sunrise effect.

Enjoy your Tequila Sunrise!
